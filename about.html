Who We Are

SkyRescue Air Ambulance is a patient-first air medical service focused on fast coordination, ICU-ready care in transit, and seamless hospital-to-hospital transfers across India.

  • 24/7 coordination with rapid dispatch and real-time updates.
  • ICU-grade equipment with trained medical teams onboard.
  • Pan-India network support with hospital coordination and ground ambulance linkage.

SkyRescue Air Ambulance is built for critical moments—where minutes matter. We operate with a safety-first mindset, combining clinical expertise with aviation-grade operational planning to move patients securely between cities and hospitals.

Our team coordinates the entire journey end-to-end: medical assessment, onboard equipment planning, ground ambulance linkage, flight clearances, and receiving-hospital handover. Families receive clear communication throughout the transfer, so the process feels guided, transparent, and calm—even during emergencies.

Whether it’s a NICU/PICU transfer, trauma case, cardiac emergency, or long-distance medical relocation, our goal is simple: deliver the right care, at the right time, with continuity and compassion.
